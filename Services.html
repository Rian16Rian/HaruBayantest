WHO ARE WE

We are founders of Hana-Bayan, a restaurant in Cagayan de Oro dedicated to bringing authentic Japanese flavors to the local community. With a passion for traditional culinary techniques, we offer freshly made sushi, ramen, tempura, and donburi, creating every dish that captures the true essence of Japan.

Together We
Are Strong

Our team at Hana-Bayan is the heart of everything we do. Each staff member brings dedication, skill, and passion to ensure the best dining experience for our customers. From our expert chefs crafting authentic Japanese dishes to our friendly servers ensuring warm hospitality, we work together to deliver true satisfaction. With commitment and enthusiasm, we maintain high standards in food quality, service, and customer satisfaction, making every visit to Hana-Bayan memorable.
